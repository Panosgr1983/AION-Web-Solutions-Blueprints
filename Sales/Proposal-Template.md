# Proposal Template

## Structure

### 1. Executive Summary (2-3 sentences)
- Their problem
- Your solution
- Expected outcome

### 2. Current Situation
- What they have now
- What's working
- What's not working
- (Use their exact words from discovery call)

### 3. Our Solution
#### Deliverables
- [ ] New website (X pages/sections)
- [ ] Mobile responsive
- [ ] Google-ready SEO
- [ ] Google Business Profile optimization
- [ ] Analytics setup
- [ ] Training / handover

#### Design Approach
- Clean, professional, trust-focused
- Dark/light mode
- Greek + English
- Real photos (not stock)

#### Technical
- Fast loading (Lighthouse 90+)
- SEO framework (Schema, OG, sitemap)
- Analytics (GA4 + Search Console + Clarity)
- Easy to update

### 4. Investment
| Item | Value |
|---|---|
| Website Design & Development | €X |
| SEO Framework | Included |
| Analytics Setup | Included |
| Google Business Optimization | Included |
| **Total Investment** | **€X** |

### 5. Timeline
| Phase | Duration |
|---|---|
| Content Collection | Week 1 |
| Design & Development | Weeks 2-3 |
| Review & Revisions | Week 4 |
| Launch | Week 5 |

### 6. What We Need From You
- Logo (vector or high-res PNG)
- 5-15 photos of your workspace
- Service list with descriptions
- Working hours
- Any existing reviews/testimonials
- GBP access (for optimization)

### 7. Next Steps
1. Review this proposal
2. Schedule a 15-min call for questions
3. Sign digital agreement
4. 50% deposit to start
5. We begin content collection

## Tone
- Professional, not salesy
- Specific, not vague
- Show you listened (reference discovery call)
- Confidence without arrogance
