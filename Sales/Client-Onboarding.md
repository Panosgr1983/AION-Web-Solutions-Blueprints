# Client Onboarding Process

## Step 1: Signed Agreement + Deposit
- Send digital agreement (deeplink / PDF)
- Receive 50% deposit
- Send payment receipt

## Step 2: Client Intake
- Send Client Intake Questionnaire
- Request all assets: logo, photos, content, reviews
- Set deadline: 1 week for content delivery
- Schedule kickoff call (30 min)

## Step 3: Kickoff Call (30 min)
- Review completed intake questionnaire
- Confirm design direction
- Set expectations: timeline, revision process, communication channel
- Answer questions
- Confirm: "We start production on [date]"

## Step 4: Production
- Designer/developer builds site based on intake + discovery
- Weekly progress update (Thursday email: 3 bullet points)
- No client interruptions during production (they had their chance in intake)

## Step 5: Review
- Share staging link
- Client has 1 week to submit revision requests
- 1 round included
- Additional rounds billed separately

## Step 6: Final Approval + Payment
- Client signs off
- Remaining 50% invoice sent
- Payment received before launch

## Step 7: Launch
- Deploy to production
- Configure domain
- Submit sitemap to Search Console
- Verify analytics are collecting data
- Send launch email: "Your site is live!"
- Provide admin access links (GA4, Clarity, Search Console)

## Step 8: Post-Launch
- Day 1: Verify everything works
- Week 1: Share first analytics screenshot
- Month 1: Send one-page performance report
- Month 3: Review + upsell maintenance retainer
