# Pricing Framework — Service Business v1

## Base Package: €1,500-2,500
The standard offering (what NextStage received):
- 1-page website with all 10 baseline sections
- Mobile responsive
- Dark/light mode
- EL/EN bilingual
- SEO framework (Schema, OG, sitemap, canonical)
- Analytics setup (GA4 + Search Console + Clarity)
- WebP image optimization
- Performance optimization (Lighthouse 90+)
- 1 round of revisions
- Documentation handover

## Premium Package: €2,500-4,000
Adds to Base:
- Case studies section (up to 3)
- Custom animations / interactions
- Extended revision rounds
- Google Business Profile full optimization
- 3 months of analytics monitoring + monthly report
- Priority support (48h response)

## Enterprise Package: €4,000-7,500
Adds to Premium:
- Multi-page (up to 5 pages)
- Blog / news section
- Custom CMS integration (if needed)
- Booking / appointment system (if applicable)
- 6 months analytics monitoring + monthly reports
- SEO content package (3 blog posts/month)
- Priority support (24h response)

## Pricing Principles
1. **Value-based, not hourly**: Price reflects the outcome, not the effort.
2. **Anchor high**: Show Premium first in conversation, Base as "a more accessible option."
3. **Never discount**: If price is an issue, reduce scope, not price.
4. **Deposit upfront**: 50% before work begins, 50% before launch.
5. **Maintenance is recurring**: Offer monthly retainer from Day 1.

## When to Walk Away
- Budget < €1,000 for a website project
- Client won't provide photos/content
- Unrealistic timeline
- Multiple decision-makers with conflicting opinions
- "My nephew can do it for €200" → let them
